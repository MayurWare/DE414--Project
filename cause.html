Project : Water fetching aid for the elderly in the rural/terrain areas

The Cause

The Cause :

Every design project is usually initiated by a need (be it a commercial requirement or a social necessity) or a problem with the product. This need or problem recognition is a key trigger for the project and it can be identified either by a product manufacturer/marketer, user, or even a product designer.

  • One of our team memberâ€™s grandparents lives in a village where the supply of water is not frequently available.
  • Many households have to travel to a nearby river (around 500-600m from the village) to bring water for their daily purpose including drinking purposes too. We thus need to minimize their efforts thereby reducing the amount of time and energy spent to fetch water using innovation by design.
  • Even after fetching water from nearby resources, the water is unsafe and unhealthy to drink. Thus we need to design water filters suitable for rural areas that cater to the needs and requirements of the elderly thereby making potable water available to them at minimal costs.
