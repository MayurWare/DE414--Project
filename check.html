Project : Water fetching aid for the elderly in the rural/terrain areas

The Check

The Check :

The check is essential to understand what requirements the product should ultimately cater to and what problems it should resolve. The check, which is a product brief is created from the design insights and is approved by the client. The product brief becomes the criterion for creating ideas and evaluating the concepts.

Here are some Checks :

  • Easy to use and carry
  • Light weight
  • Sturdy and Durable
  • Should carry large amount of water
  • Handle for easy lifting of the container(s)
  • No pain in the body
  • Easy to fill
  • Lid to avoid leakage
  • Insulated
  • All terrain use
  • Should be able to use with wet hands, should not be slippery
  • Easy to balance while carrying the container(s)
