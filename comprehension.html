Project : Water fetching aid for the elderly in the rural/terrain areas

The Comprehension

The Comprehension :

The designer analyses all the information gathered and methodically lists out his observations and perceptions of the issues involved and the causes behind. The design insights thus arrived form the basis for the design intervention.

Here are some findings after doing 'The Context' part :

  • The pain experienced in shoulders and legs by the elderly needs to be reduced.
  • The water carrying technique needs to be modified so as to completely reduce the load on the head due to the containers.
  • The number of round trips need to be reduced so that less time is spent in fetching water.
  • The product should be cheap or affordable by the elderly in rural areas.
  • Preferably the material used should be recyclable.
  • The material should prevent the formation of fungus inside the container to avoid contamination of water.
  • The container should be easy to maintain by the elderly so that they don't face problems in the long run.
  • Airtight containers should be preferred.
  • Elderly should be able to easily lift the container.
  • If a filter is provided with the container it would be advantageous.
