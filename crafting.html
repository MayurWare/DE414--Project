Project : Water fetching aid for the elderly in the rural/terrain areas

The Crafting

The Crafting :

The final concept is further refined against various criteria like the use of material and manufacturing limitations. Depending on the user profile, the designer at this stage creates several style options and zeroes in on the one that addresses the aesthetics concerns in the best manner. The final product design is showcased by creating small-scale mock-up models, CAD models or working prototypes. The final product which meets the product brief conditions is then taken up for mass production.

The Final Product :

Product Specifications/Features :

  • Cost : Roughly around 500 rupees considering the cost of each and every sub part
  • Bucket with Lid :
    This is done to avoid leakage of water while carrying it across different terrains
  • Plastic Buckets :
    This will help to reduce weight, make it more durable and strong and also provide insulation
  • Detachable Buckets :
    Unique detachable attachment of the buckets to the chains allows the user to easily remove the buckets whenever required. The user also can attach any size buckets of his own as per requirement
  • 3 Metal chain attachment on either sides :
    This provides an optimal combination of stability while carrying water coupled with strength and durability
  • Chains easily detachable from rods :
    The hook allows the user to easily detach the chains from the upper rod.This helps the user to easily store it
  • Neck Rest/Support :
    This provides the user comfort and stability while carrying water. By using this, any pain on the neck due to the rod will be eliminated
  • Handle :
    The two additional handles on either side serve the purpose of easy lifting
  • Bamboo/Plastic Rod :
    This allows the main upper rod to be flexible and durable along with light weight
