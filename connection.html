The Connection

The Connection :

The last but not the least important concern is the connection, the new product makes with the consumer. This connection or the bond between the user and the product builds up only when the user is satisfied with the product performance and begins to cherish it, which intern creates a demand for the product. In fact, it is the customer satisfaction that makes the winning product and completes the most important criterion for innovation. The following chapters that portray the new Post Box design and development have been grouped under seven sections representing the seven concerns listed above.

For the Connection? Feedback purpose, we interviewed Mr. Bipin Seth and Ms. Josna Pandey again.
